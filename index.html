We Empower Communities through Sustainable Agriculture & Conservation

Mundu Agricultural Environmental and Educational Organization (MAEEO), established in 2003, is a non-profit NGO based in Machakos, Kenya. Focused on the marginalized and drought-prone regions of Machakos, Makueni, and Kitui counties, MAEEO works directly with grassroots community groups, particularly women and youth, to implement sustainable projects in ecological agriculture, environmental conservation, and community empowerment.

Sustainable Solutions

We drive lasting change by empowering communities to implement sustainable projects that improve their livelihoods.
